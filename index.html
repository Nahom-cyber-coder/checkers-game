How to Play Neon Checkers

Moving Pieces

Pieces can only move diagonally forward to an adjacent dark square. Regular pieces can move one square at a time.

Capturing Opponent Pieces

If an opponent's piece is adjacent to your piece and the square immediately beyond it is empty, you can jump over the opponent's piece to capture it. Multiple captures are allowed in a single turn if possible.

Kinging a Piece

When a piece reaches the farthest row (the "king row"), it is crowned a king. Kings can move both forward and backward diagonally.

Winning the Game

The game is won by capturing all of your opponent's pieces or by blocking them so they cannot make any moves.
